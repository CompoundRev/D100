Frequently Asked Questions

How much do dental implants cost?

▼

The investment varies depending on your specific needs, but most patients are surprised to find it's more affordable than they expected—especially with our flexible financing options. During your free consultation, we'll give you a clear, transparent quote with no hidden fees.

Will the procedure hurt?

▼

Most patients report feeling little to no discomfort during the procedure, especially with our advanced sedation options. Afterward, any soreness is typically mild and manageable with over-the-counter pain relief. Many patients are back to normal activities within a few days.

How long does the process take?

▼

With our same-day teeth technology, you can walk in and leave with a functional, beautiful smile the same day. The implants will fully integrate with your bone over the next few months, but you'll have teeth immediately.

What if I've been told I don't have enough bone for implants?

▼

We specialize in advanced techniques like All-on-X that work even when bone loss is significant. Many patients who were told "no" by other dentists have been successfully treated here. We'll know for sure after your 3D imaging consultation.

Do you take insurance?

Yes. We work with most major insurance providers and will help you maximize your benefits. We also offer in-house financing and payment plans to make treatment accessible.
