Frequently Asked Questions

How painful is the All-on-X procedure?

Most patients are surprised at how manageable the discomfort is. We offer sedation options to keep you completely relaxed during the procedure, and most people describe the recovery as less painful than a tooth extraction. You'll receive detailed aftercare instructions and pain management support.

Will my new teeth look fake or obvious?

Not at all. Your restoration is custom-designed to match your facial structure, age, and natural coloring. We create teeth that look like beautiful, natural teeth - not overly white veneers or obvious dentures. Friends will notice you look great but won't be able to pinpoint why.

Can I really afford this?

We offer multiple financing options with low down payments and flexible monthly plans. Many patients find the monthly investment comparable to what they were spending on denture maintenance, repairs, and special foods. We'll create a payment plan that fits your budget.

How long will the results last?

With proper care (regular brushing and dental check-ups), All-on-X implants typically last 20+ years, often a lifetime. The implants themselves are designed to be permanent, and the restoration is incredibly durable. This is a long-term investment in your quality of life.

Am I too old for this procedure?

Age alone is rarely a disqualifying factor. We've successfully treated patients well into their 80s. What matters is your overall health and bone structure, which we'll evaluate during your consultation. Many older patients tell us they wish they'd done it sooner.
