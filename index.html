Frequently Asked Questions

Will my results look natural or like obvious hair plugs?

▼

When FUE is performed correctly by an experienced surgeon, your results should be completely undetectable. Dr. Yazdan designs each hairline to match your age, face shape, and natural hair characteristics, and places grafts at the correct angle and density to mimic natural growth. The goal is for even your barber not to know you had work done.

How long until I see final results?

▼

Transplanted hair typically sheds within 2-3 weeks (this is normal), then begins regrowing around 3-4 months. You'll see progressive improvement over 8-12 months, with final results visible around 12-18 months as the hair matures and thickens.

How much does FUE hair transplant cost?

▼

Cost depends on the number of grafts needed to achieve your goals. During your consultation, Dr. Yazdan will provide a transparent quote based on your specific case. Financing options are available to make the investment manageable.

Is the procedure painful?

▼

The procedure is performed under local anesthesia, so you won't feel pain during surgery. Most patients describe mild discomfort afterward, manageable with prescribed medication. Recovery is typically smooth with proper aftercare.

Can people tell I had something done right after the procedure?

▼

There will be some redness and small scabs in the recipient area for the first 7-10 days. Most patients can return to work within a week, and any visible signs fade quickly. By two weeks, most people won't notice anything unusual.
