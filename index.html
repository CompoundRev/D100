Frequently Asked Questions

Will people know I had a facelift?

▼

When performed by skilled, board-certified surgeons, a facelift should look completely natural. Our goal is for people to say "you look amazing" without being able to pinpoint why. We specialize in subtle, age-appropriate rejuvenation that restores rather than transforms.

What is recovery really like?

▼

Most patients return to social activities within 2-3 weeks. Bruising and swelling are temporary and manageable. We'll give you a detailed recovery plan and support you throughout the healing process.

Am I too young or too old for a facelift?

▼

There's no "right" age, only the right time for you. We've successfully performed facelifts on patients in their 40s through their 70s. What matters most is skin quality, overall health, and your aesthetic goals.

What if I'm worried about scarring?

▼

Our surgeons use advanced techniques that place incisions in natural contours and hairlines, making them virtually invisible once healed. We prioritize both results and discretion.

Can a facelift be combined with other procedures?

▼

Absolutely. Many patients combine facelifts with eyelid surgery, brow lifts, or laser treatments for comprehensive facial rejuvenation. We'll discuss all options during your consultation.

Is it safe at my age?

▼

Facelift surgery is safe for healthy adults of varying ages. During your consultation, we'll review your medical history and ensure you're an excellent candidate. Safety is our top priority.
