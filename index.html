How do I know if I'm a good candidate for rhinoplasty?

▼

Will my results look natural, or will people know I had surgery?

▼
Dr. Meade's philosophy is to enhance, not erase. His goal is to create results that look like they've always belonged on your face - balanced, proportional, and beautifully natural. Most patients hear comments like "you look refreshed" rather than "you had work done."

How long is the recovery, and when can I return to work?

▼
Most patients return to work within 7-10 days. Swelling and bruising improve significantly within the first two weeks. You'll see noticeable improvement by 4-6 weeks, with continued refinement over the following months. Final results are typically visible by 12 months.

Can rhinoplasty improve my breathing as well as my appearance?

▼
Absolutely. Dr. Meade frequently combines functional corrections (like septoplasty) with aesthetic refinement in a single procedure, so you don't have to choose between breathing better and looking better.

Will I lose my ethnic identity or unique features?

▼
Not at all. Dr. Meade takes an ethnically sensitive approach that respects and preserves your cultural identity. The goal is refinement that honors your heritage, not transformation that erases it.
