Frequently Asked Questions

Will my results look natural, or will people be able to tell I had surgery?

▼

Dr. Morin specializes in creating natural, harmonious results that complement your facial features. The goal is for people to think you look great, not to notice you had surgery. He avoids cookie-cutter approaches and focuses on what fits your face.

How long is the recovery, and when can I get back to work?

▼

Most patients return to work within 7-10 days. You'll have some swelling and bruising initially, but Dr. Morin uses advanced ultrasound techniques that minimize trauma and speed up recovery. Full results develop over several months as swelling gradually subsides.

Does rhinoplasty hurt?

▼

Discomfort is typically mild and well-managed with medication. Most patients describe feeling pressure or congestion rather than pain. Dr. Morin prioritizes your comfort throughout the entire process.

Can rhinoplasty improve my breathing as well as my appearance?

▼

Absolutely. Dr. Morin often combines aesthetic improvements with functional corrections like fixing a deviated septum. You can breathe better AND look better—it's not one or the other.

How much does rhinoplasty cost?

▼

Costs vary based on the complexity of your case. During your consultation, you'll receive a detailed breakdown. We work with financing partners to make your transformation accessible and manageable.
