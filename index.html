Frequently Asked Questions

Is the procedure painful?

▼

Most patients are surprised by how comfortable the process is. We offer sedation options to keep you completely relaxed during surgery, and post-procedure discomfort is typically manageable with over-the-counter pain medication. Many patients return to normal activities within 2-4 days.

What if I don't have enough bone for implants?

▼

That's the beauty of All-on-4 - it's specifically designed for patients with bone loss. The implants are placed at precise angles to maximize your existing bone structure, often eliminating the need for bone grafting entirely.

How much do All-on-4 implants cost?

▼

Treatment costs vary based on your specific needs, but we provide transparent pricing during your consultation. We also offer flexible financing options and work with you to maximize insurance benefits. Many patients find monthly payments more affordable than they expected.

Will my implants look natural?

▼

Yes. Your prosthetic teeth are custom-designed to match your facial structure, age, and desired appearance. The goal is a smile that looks and feels like your own - not fake or obviously artificial.

How do I know if I'm a candidate for All-on-4?

▼

The best way to find out is through a consultation with Dr. Betts. We'll perform a comprehensive evaluation, including 3D imaging, to determine if All-on-4 is right for you and create a personalized treatment plan.
