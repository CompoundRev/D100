Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know if I'm a good candidate for rhinoplasty?

Most healthy adults who are unhappy with the shape, size, or function of their nose are good candidates. During your consultation, Dr. Patel will evaluate your facial structure, discuss your goals, and determine if rhinoplasty is right for you.

Will my results look natural, or will people know I had surgery?

Dr. Patel's goal is always to enhance your natural beauty—not create an obvious "nose job" look. The best rhinoplasty results are the ones where people notice you look great, but can't pinpoint why.

How long is the recovery?

Most patients return to work within 1-2 weeks. Swelling and bruising typically subside within 2-3 weeks, though final results can take up to a year to fully settle. Dr. Patel will give you a detailed recovery timeline during your consultation.

What if I've had rhinoplasty before and wasn't happy with the results?

Dr. Patel specializes in revision rhinoplasty and has the craniofacial training needed to correct previous surgeries. Revision cases are more complex, but they're also where his expertise truly shines.

How much does rhinoplasty cost?

Cost varies based on the complexity of your case. During your consultation, Dr. Patel will provide a detailed quote and discuss financing options to make your procedure as accessible as possible.

What happens during the consultation?

You'll meet with Dr. Patel to discuss your goals, medical history, and concerns. He'll examine your facial structure, explain what's possible, and create a personalized surgical plan. There's no pressure—just honest guidance.

Is rhinoplasty painful?

Most patients report mild discomfort rather than significant pain. Dr. Patel will prescribe medication to manage any discomfort during recovery, and most people are surprised by how manageable the process is.
