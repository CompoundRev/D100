Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it really take to look normal after rhinoplasty?

▼

Most patients feel comfortable in public within 10-14 days once the splint and visible bruising are gone. Swelling gradually reduces over 6-12 months, but you'll look like yourself - just refined - much sooner than you think.

Can you fix both my breathing problems and the shape of my nose?

▼

Absolutely. Dr. Katrib's dual board certification in both otolaryngology and facial plastic surgery means he routinely combines functional septoplasty with aesthetic rhinoplasty in a single procedure - addressing both concerns at once.

I want refinement, but I don't want to lose my ethnic features. Is that possible?

▼

Yes. Dr. Katrib specializes in ethnic rhinoplasty that honors your cultural identity while achieving the balance and proportion you desire. The goal is to enhance your natural beauty, not erase your heritage.
