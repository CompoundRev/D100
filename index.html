Frequently Asked Questions

How is EVO ICL different from LASIK?

▼

LASIK reshapes your cornea with a laser, permanently removing tissue. EVO ICL places a soft lens behind your iris without touching your cornea. It's reversible, doesn't cause dry eye, and works for prescriptions LASIK can't safely treat.

Does it hurt?

▼

The procedure takes about 15 minutes per eye under numbing drops. Most patients report feeling slight pressure but no pain. You'll go home the same day and see clearly within hours.

What if my vision changes later in life?

▼

That's the beauty of EVO ICL - it's removable and replaceable. If your prescription changes or new technology emerges, we can adjust. You're never locked in.

How long does EVO ICL last?

▼

The lens is designed to stay in place permanently, but unlike LASIK, it can be removed or exchanged if needed. Many patients have had their lenses for over 10 years with perfect results.

What's the recovery like?

▼

Most patients see clearly within 24 hours and return to work in 1-2 days. You'll avoid strenuous exercise and swimming for a week, but normal activities resume quickly.

Am I too old (or too young) for EVO ICL?

▼

EVO ICL is FDA-approved for patients 21-45, but Dr. Tooma evaluates every case individually. If you're outside that range, there may still be excellent options for you.
