Will people be able to tell I had a hair transplant?

▼

No. Dr. Bauman's FUE technique creates results so natural that even intimate partners and barbers can't detect the work. Your hairline is custom-designed to match your age, facial structure, and natural growth patterns—it looks like the hair you were born with because it is your hair.

How long is the recovery? Will I need to take weeks off work?

▼

Most patients return to work within 2-3 days and resume exercise within a week. There are no stitches or staples. You'll have some minor redness and tiny scabs that resolve quickly. This is minimally invasive with dramatically faster healing than outdated strip methods.

Is the procedure painful?

▼

No. The procedure is performed under local anesthesia, and most patients report minimal discomfort during and after. Many even watch movies or work on their laptops during the procedure. Post-procedure discomfort is mild and easily managed.

How do I know if I'm a good candidate?

▼

The best way to know is through a personal consultation with Dr. Bauman. He'll assess your hair loss pattern, donor area, overall health, and aesthetic goals to determine if FUE is right for you—and design a personalized plan if it is.

Will I need multiple procedures, or is this really a one-time solution?

▼

Most patients achieve their desired results in one procedure. Some may choose an enhancement session later for added density, or may need a touch-up years later if natural age-related thinning occurs in non-transplanted areas. Dr. Bauman will give you an honest assessment during your consultation.
