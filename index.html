Frequently Asked Questions

How is Alma TED different from Rogaine or other topical treatments?

↓

Rogaine relies on daily chemical application that many people find messy and inconsistent. Alma TED uses ultrasound technology combined with topical growth factors to penetrate deeper into the scalp, stimulating follicles at a cellular level. It's a completely different mechanism - and it doesn't require daily commitment.

Will people be able to tell I've had treatment?

↓

No. The results look completely natural because you're regrowing your own hair, not transplanting plugs or wearing anything artificial. Most clients say people notice they look "refreshed" but can't pinpoint why.

How long before I see results?

↓

Most patients notice visible improvements within 90 days (approximately 3 treatments). Some see changes even sooner. This is significantly faster than medications like Propecia, which can take 6-12 months.

Is it painful?

↓

Not at all. Alma TED uses acoustic sound waves and feels like a gentle massage on the scalp. There's no downtime, and you can return to normal activities immediately after treatment.

How much does it cost?

↓

Pricing depends on your specific treatment plan, which we'll discuss during your consultation. We believe in transparent pricing with no hidden fees - you'll know exactly what to expect before committing to anything.

Is this safe?

↓

Yes. Alma TED is FDA-cleared and administered under the supervision of our Medical Director, Leslie Poirier. It's a non-invasive treatment with an excellent safety profile and no systemic side effects.
