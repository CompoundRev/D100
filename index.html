▼

How long is the recovery, and when can I return to work?

▼

Most patients return to work within 7-10 days. While some residual swelling continues to resolve over several months, the visible recovery period is much shorter than many people expect. Dr. Hamrah will provide a detailed recovery timeline during your consultation.

Does rhinoplasty hurt?

▼

Most patients report that rhinoplasty is less painful than they anticipated. You'll receive comprehensive pain management instructions, and any discomfort is typically well-controlled with prescribed medication during the first few days.

Can rhinoplasty improve my breathing?

▼

Yes. Many rhinoplasty procedures address both aesthetic and functional concerns. If you have breathing difficulties due to a deviated septum or other structural issues, Dr. Hamrah can often correct these while enhancing the appearance of your nose.

How much does rhinoplasty cost?

The cost varies depending on the complexity of your case and your specific goals. During your consultation, you'll receive a transparent, detailed cost breakdown with no hidden fees. Financing options are also available.
