Will it hurt?

▼
The procedure is performed under anesthesia, so you won't feel pain during treatment. Most patients report mild discomfort afterward that's easily managed with over-the-counter pain relief.

How much does All-on-4 cost?

▼
Cost varies based on your specific needs, but we provide transparent pricing during your consultation. We also offer flexible financing through Cherry to make payments manageable.

How long do All-on-4 implants last?

▼
With proper care, All-on-4 implants can last 20-30 years or longer. You'll care for them just like natural teeth - brushing, flossing, and regular checkups.

Can I eat normally with All-on-4 implants?

▼
Yes. Once healed, you can eat steak, apples, corn on the cob, and all the foods you've been avoiding. Your bite force is restored to near-natural levels.
