Frequently Asked Questions

Will I look natural, or will it be obvious I had surgery?

▼

Dr. Horowitz's entire surgical philosophy is built around natural elegance. His LiteLift technique is specifically designed to refresh and restore—not create an artificial, "worked on" appearance. Patients consistently report that people notice they look great, but can't pinpoint why.

How long is the recovery, and when can I return to work?

▼

Most patients feel comfortable returning to work and social activities within 2-3 weeks. Visible bruising and swelling are typically minimal by week two. Dr. Horowitz uses advanced techniques that significantly reduce downtime compared to traditional facelifts.

How long will my results last?

▼

Dr. Horowitz's facelift results typically last 10-15 years, making it a true long-term investment. Unlike fillers or non-surgical treatments that require constant maintenance, a well-performed facelift delivers lasting transformation.

I'm worried about scarring. Will it be visible?

▼

Dr. Horowitz places incisions strategically along natural contours and hairlines where they're virtually imperceptible once healed. His meticulous technique and decades of experience ensure scars are minimal and well-hidden.

Is a facelift worth the investment compared to non-surgical treatments?

▼

If you've been maintaining results with fillers, Botox, and other treatments, you're likely spending thousands per year with temporary outcomes. A facelift is a one-time investment that delivers lasting transformation—often proving more cost-effective over time while providing far superior results.
