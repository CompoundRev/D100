Frequently Asked Questions

Will I look "done" or obviously surgical?

▼

Absolutely not. Dr. Jacobs specializes in natural-looking results that refresh your appearance without changing your fundamental features. The goal is for people to say "you look amazing" - not "did you have work done?"

How long is the recovery, and when can I return to work?

▼

Most patients return to work within 10-14 days, with bruising and swelling largely resolved by week two. You'll receive detailed post-operative protocols to optimize healing and minimize visible downtime.

How long will my results last?

▼

A well-executed facelift typically lasts 10-15 years. While you'll continue to age naturally, you'll maintain a more youthful baseline than if you hadn't had the procedure. Many patients say they still look better a decade later than they did before surgery.

Is the procedure painful?

▼

You'll be under anesthesia during surgery and feel no pain. Post-operatively, discomfort is typically mild and well-managed with prescribed medication. Most patients describe it as tightness rather than pain.

What makes Dr. Jacobs different from other plastic surgeons?

▼

His commitment to conservative, natural results combined with proven surgical expertise and genuinely personalized care. You're not a number here, you're a patient with unique goals, and your treatment plan reflects that.

How do I know if I'm a good candidate?

▼

The best candidates are in good general health, have realistic expectations, and are bothered by visible signs of facial aging that non-surgical treatments can't adequately address. Your consultation will determine candidacy and discuss what's achievable for your unique situation.
